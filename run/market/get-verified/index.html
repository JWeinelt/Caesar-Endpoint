Developer verification - what is it and how to get it?

What's the mean of verification

With a verified developer profile you provide users more professionality and they know, that they can trust you and your work. As a verified developer, your plugins will be shown up and the landing page of this site and users can filter by verified developers in search.

What do I need to get verified?

As a verified developer you are a person of trust. Therefore, you need to fill this requirements:

  • Your plugins have an average download rate of 1,000.
  • All of your work is licensed under GPL v3 or MIT and is available on GitHub.
  • Your account must be at least 4 weeks old.
  • You must have 20 or more posts in the forum.
  • A valid passport to verify you are a real person.

Your benefits as a verified developer

  • You get the "verified developer" badge.
  • You are trusted by the community.
  • You are allowed to publish paid plugins.
